Can you survive Lobotomy Corporation?

You are a new agent at Lobotomy Corporation. Things are going fine until the power suddenly cuts out.
Strange creatures known as Abnormalities are wandering the halls. Screams echo throughout the facility.
Can you survive until the lights turn back on or will you be yet another corpse littering the floor?

You hear from someone that the only way to turn the lights back on is to interact with those Abnormalities that fill the facility. Many of them are deadly and can kill you in seconds, others make your body a host for some cruel infection, disease, or parasite, and others are horrible enough to drive you mad at a glance. A higher up shouts at you to get moving and get to work.

In this hall there are 3 containment units. All of the doors are shut tight and you can't tell what's within. This department is home to some of the weaker Abnormalities. However, this doesn't mean that they aren't dangerous.
